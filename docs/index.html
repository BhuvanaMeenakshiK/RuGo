Experience the magic of RuGo

Welcome to a virtual space where one can create, visualize, and share Rube Goldberg machines. Unlike the physical world, RuGo allows people to prototype their creations in a sandbox environment where space and resources are not constrained.

Gameplay Features

Realistic Physics

RuGo players can select in insert a variety of different physics-based gadgets that can interact when one another in a realistic manner.

Load and Save Scenes

Users can easily save existing creations or load and edit old scenes that they may have created.

Easy Reset

In the real world, reseting every object is often one of the most tedious parts of creating a Rube Goldberg machine. But in RuGo, resetting everything is as easy as one button click.

Cool Sound Effects

Many of the effects in the game feature realistic sounds, like the sound of a ball rolling across a floor, or a fan blowing air, or even a line of dominos toppling into one another.

RuGo Gadgets

RuGo gives you the oppurtunity to play and build with a number of different gadgets, each complete with their own unique properties.
Here are just a couple of the amazing devices you will get to use in your virtual world.

Domino Path Gadget

Just point and click to draw an elaborate path of dominos across the room without having to take even a single step!

Ball Gadgets

The quintessential gadget. Comes in light and heavy versions and can be used to knock down dominos, trigger the cannon, and much more!

Ramp Gadget

With the ramp, you can easily create a seemless path between two different heights for the ball gadget!     

Box Gadget

Useful for placing almost any gadget one. You can also compete with your friends to see who can create the tallest stack of boxes!

Cannon Gadget

Use this gadget to fire tiny projectiles at other objects in your scene, and with the built-in trajectory, aiming the cannon is super easy!

Floating Platform Gadget

Introduce verticality into your scene with floating platform gadgets that can be placed at any angle or height anywhere in the room!

Fan Gadget

By placing the fan in front of any other gadget in your scene, watch the other gadgets getting blown away by the fan's wind!

Airplane Gadget

Place the airplane gadget anywhere in your scene and watch it take off in front of you and actively interacting with other gadgets!

Goal Gadget

Place the goal in your scene marking end of the level triggering the celebration event after watching your very own RuGo in action!
